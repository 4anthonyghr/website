About

I am a software architect and principal engineer with 25+ years of experience building large-scale enterprise systems. For 14 years I served as a software architect at Carnegie Mellon University, where I led the architecture of the S3 Student Information System — a half-million-line Java codebase serving the full academic and financial lifecycle of one of the most complex research universities in the world.

My background spans enterprise Java, distributed systems, cloud infrastructure, and data integration. I have built production software in domains ranging from public health informatics and pharmaceutical compliance to defense research and higher education. I am equally comfortable driving technical decisions at the architectural level and writing production code.

I am currently available for full-time Principal Engineer, Staff Engineer, or Software Architect roles — remote preferred.
